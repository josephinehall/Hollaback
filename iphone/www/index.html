About

Back

Hollaback is a movement to end street harassment. We believe that everyone has the right to feel safe, confident, and sexy when they walk down the street.

By holla'ing back, you bring attention to street harassment and make a powerful case for why it must end. When the time comes to hollaback, you will have a choice to hollaback with or without a picture. The picture helps to tell a story, and can include anything from the turd himself, to your shoes or the street scene. If you don't feel safe, don't take one. The next step is to identify from a list the type of harassment. Set your location and enter your story details. Then, viola! Your hollaback has been submitted.

Once submitted you will receive an email asking you to tell the rest of your story. If you submitted your hollaback in error, this is the time to tell us. Your full story will be reviewed and mapped on your local hollaback site, where it will inspire the world to take action and end street harassment.

Don't just walk on... Hollaback!

This app was developed in partnership Hollaback International by Amy Palamountain and Josephine Hall. Any problems with this app should be sent to info@ihollaback.org
